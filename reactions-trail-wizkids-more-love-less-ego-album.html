Reactions Trail Wizkid's 'More Love, Less Ego' Album

Publish date: 2024-02-05

Reactions have trailed the long-awaited album release ‘More Love, Less Ego’ of Nigerian Grammy award-winning singer, Wizkid.

The new album, which is coming two years after ‘Made in Lagos’, have climbed to the number spot on both NG Apple and NG iTunes album charts respectively.

Before the album’s release, Wizkid dropped some singles, including “Bad to Me” and “Money & Love.”

The More Love, Less Ego album features both local and foreign musicians including Ayra Starr, Skepta, Naira Marley, Don Toliver as well as Jamaica-born Skillibeng and Shenseea

Arya Starr was featured on the fourth track, ‘2 Sugar’ while Jamaican artists Skillibeng and Shensea appeared on the sixth track ‘Slip N Slide’. American rapper Don Toliver featured on the track ‘Special’.

Tracks on the album include ‘Money and love’, ‘Balance’, ‘Bad to me, ‘Everyday’, ‘Deep’, ‘Flower Pads’, ‘Pressure’, ‘Plenty Loving’ and ‘Frames (Who’s going to know).
