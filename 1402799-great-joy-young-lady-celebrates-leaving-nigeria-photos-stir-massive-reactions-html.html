Joy as young lady celebrates leaving Nigeria, photos stir massive reactions

Publish date: 2024-03-13

- A young Nigerian lady has celebrated a new achievement in her life

- The lady, Oluwabunmi Amosu, shared photos as she was excited at leaving the country for Canada

- Congratulations poured in for her as many said it is a dream come true for the first-class graduate

PAY ATTENTION: Join Legit.ng Telegram channel! Never miss important updates!

An overjoyed young lady has taken to social media to share a recent success she recorded in her life.

Oluwabunmi Amosu posted on her Linkedln wall on Saturday, February 13, that she is no longer in Nigeria as she left the country for Canada.

Amosu who hailed from Badagry in Lagos state shared pictures of herself with her luggage as she touched down in the North America country.

She wrote:

"From Badagry to Canada"Your dreams are valid"Proudly a MasterCard Scholar @ McGill University."

Nigerians, as well as other users of the platform, filled the comment section of the Lagos state university first class graduate with good wishes and congratulatory messages.

Michael Lelecy Yahaya wrote:

"Congratulations. God is wonderful. God has answered your prayers."These congratulations will plenty this year."You packed all the whole bags. 'You no leave one in Nigeria at all."

Emmanuel Sewanu Dansu said:

"Congratulations, Oluwabunmi Amosu. I remember your BSc. project defense just like yesterday! You have it in you! Go girl! Make LASU proud, and make Badagry proud too. Best wishes from Badagry."

Oluwaseun Abass reacted:

"Your are welcome to the land of cold and great opportunities. Go and explore."
