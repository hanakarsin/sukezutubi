Isabella Blake-Thomas - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-10

Well known for her role as Violet-Elizabeth Bott in the 2010 TV serial Just William, she has also appeared in the soap opera Doctors and in the sci-fi phenomenon Doctor Who. She also appeared in two episodes of Shameless and an episode of Nickelodeon's Game Shakers in 2015 and in 2016, she appeared in ABC's Once Upon A Time.

Before Fame

She got her start at the age of five as Lily-Rose in Green Balloon Club which has become known as one of her more prominent roles. She followed this by appearing in a minor role for Enid.

Trivia

She appeared in an episode of Midsommer Murders called "Schooled in Murder" as the character Holly in 2013. In addition to her acting, she is also a singer/songwriter and aerialist.

Family Life

She was born in Wandsworth, London to her parents Charlie and Elizabeth. She has a maltese puppy named Chai.

Associated With

She played Izzie in Johnny English: Reborn, starring Rowan Atkinson.
