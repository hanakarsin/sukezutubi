Moment Tinubu Arrived Stadium In Jos To Officially Kick-off 2023 Presidential Campaign (Video)

Publish date: 2024-02-11

The presidential candidate of the All Progressives Congress (APC), Bola Tinubu has arrived the Rwang Pam township stadium in Jos, Plateau State to kick off his campaigns.

Naija News understands Tinubu arrived at the venue of the campaign kick-off in the company of his running mate, Senator Kashim Shettima.

The APC candidate was greeted with shouts from the crowd who had already stormed the stadium before his arrival.

As Tinubu made his way to the VIP stand, he was also seen in the video waving to the ecstatic crowd.

Watch the video below.

As earlier reported by Naija News, Tinubu arrived in Jos, Plateau State capital, for the kick-off of his campaign rally scheduled to hold on Tuesday.

Tinubu and his entourage were received by the State Governor, Simon Lalong, who doubles as the Director General of the party’s campaign council.

Some other party leaders expected at the official flag-off are President Muhammadu Buhari, Vice President Yemi Osinbajo, Governors, Senate President Ahmad Lawan, APC National Chairman Senator Abdullahi Adamu among other leaders and members of the party across the nation.
