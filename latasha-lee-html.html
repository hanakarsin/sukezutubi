LaTasha Lee - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-04-10

Retro soul singer headlining the group LaTasha Lee & The BlackTies. She first rose to fame in 2012 after she made it to the top 50 on the reality competition series The X Factor. The following year, she released her debut self-titled album.

Before Fame

After years of wholeheartedly pursuing her career in performance, at twenty she was signed to the Carnival Beats production company.

Trivia

In 2016, she made an appearance in the second season of the ABC series American Crime.

Family Life

Her mother's name is Michelle Crayton.

Associated With

She appears alongside Timothy Hutton and Felicity Huffman in the second season of American Crime.
