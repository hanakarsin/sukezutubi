Dunsin Oyekan, Actor Ademola Mourns Late Cleric, Baba Olowere

Publish date: 2024-04-21

Nigerian gospel singer, Dunsin Oyekan and Nollywood actor, Muyiwa Ademola on Thursday paid tributes to the late Christ Apostolic Church (CAC) cleric, Prophet Michael Olowere better known as Baba Olowere.

Naija News reports that the clergyman died on Wednesday evening at his residence in Ibadan.

Before his death, Baba Olowere was one of the CAC’s most respected ministers.

Taking to his Instagram page, Muyiwa recounted his only meeting with the clergyman, revealing that Olowere gave him a prophecy that came to pass.

The actor also described his life as one well spent.

“Baba Olowere has gone to rest. A life well spent. I met Baba once and what he told me then actually came to pass. It is a life well spent. Sunre o Baba oninure,” the actor wrote.

 Dunsin Oyekan, on the other paid tribute to the late prophet via his Instagram page by sharing the late clergyman’s photo.

The singer shared the photo with the caption: “LAST MAN STANDING!!! This is truly “Rest in POWER”

“You took me not only as a son but also as a friend. Very concerned about every detail of my Life, family and ministry. I will really miss you.

“JESUS!!! POWER!!!Lived a full life of over 100 years in vitality, strength and Grace!!! I honour you Prophet Olowere.”
