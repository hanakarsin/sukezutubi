Heartbreaking Photos: Massive Earthquake Hits Nepal

Publish date: 2024-07-08

Nepal has been hit by a magnitude-7.8 earthquake.

According to reports, this is the worst quake to have ever hit the Himalayan nation in over 80 years. Rescue teams have reached the area and are searching for survivors. A police spokesman made it known that the death toll has reached 449.
