Neo, Angel, Pere, Uriel, , Princess, Others Return For BBNaija All Stars

Publish date: 2024-06-05

Some former Big Brother housemates have returned for the All-Stars edition of the reality show, which premiered on July 23.

Recall that the organisers of the Big Brother Naija reality show announced that Ebuka Obi Uchendu would serve as the host of Season 8, tagged “Big Brother Naija All-Stars.”

The Al-Stars season will include selected former housemates from Pepper Dem, Double Wahala, See Gobe and other editions.

However, some of the housemates who have returned to the show included Alex, Neo, Uriel, Angel, , Seyi Awolowo, Pere, Uriel and Princess among others.
