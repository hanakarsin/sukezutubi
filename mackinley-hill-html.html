Mackinley Hill - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-03-10

IMG signed model and social media personality who has more than 190,000 followers on Instagram. She graced the cover of Z!nk magazine in 2017. She's traveled to the continent Europe. 

Before Fame

She was discovered to be a model when she was 13 years old through the help of her older sister.

Trivia

She and her siblings created a channel together called The Hill Sisters and Chase. She collaborated with the fashion, beauty, Influencer style & travel magazine Modeliste Magazine in 2022.

Family Life

Her sister, Logan Rae is a fashion photographer. She has a brother named Chase, who is also a model.

Associated With

Her sister Taylor Hill became a Victoria's Secret model.
