Oscars 2020: Full list of nominees and winners

Publish date: 2024-03-09

For a long period now, Academy Awards, popularly known as the Oscars has remained the world’s most coveted award. This year, it has not been any different as Oscars 2020 saw the crème de la crème of the industry take home the trophies and world-wide recognition.

The 92nd Academy Awards is a sure indicator that the Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences (AMPAS) has maintained steady growth over the years. The ceremony took place on February 9th 2020 at Dolby Theatre in Los Angeles, California, for the 18th consecutive year. Although the nominations had several names, only the Oscars winners in 2020 walked home with the awards.

Oscars 2020 winners list

If you did not get the chance to watch the Oscar-winning actors, here is the list of the academy awards categories with nominees and winners.

1. Oscars winner for best picture

  • Marriage Story - Noah Baumbach and David Heyman
  • 1917 - Sam Mendes, Pippa Harris, Jayne-Ann Tenggren and Callum McDougall
  • Once Upon A Time in Hollywood - David Heyman, Shannon McIntosh, and Quentin Tarantino
  • Jojo Rabbit - Carthew Neal, Taika Waititi and Chelsea Winstanley
  • Joker - Todd Phillips, Bradley Cooper, and Emma Tillinger Koskoff
  • Little Women - Amy Pascal
  • Ford v Ferrari - Peter Chernin, Jenno Topping, and James Mangold
  • The Irishman - Martin Scorsese, Robert De Niro, Jane Rosenthal, and Emma Tillinger Koskoff
  • Parasite - Kwak Sin Ae and Bong Joon Ho - Winner
