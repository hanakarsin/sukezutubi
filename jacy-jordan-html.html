Jacy Jordan - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-07

Dancer known for her appearance on So You Think You Can Dance and her incredible recovery story after her leg was horribly damaged in a car accident. 

Before Fame

She posted her first tweet on February 3rd, 2012. 

Trivia

After the accident that mangled her leg, the doctors told her she would never walk again, much less dance. 

Family Life

She is the sister of collegiate gymnast Jessie Jordan. 

Associated With

Judges Paula Abdul and Jason Derulo moved Jacy onto the next round to star in Season 12 of "So You Think You Can Dance."
