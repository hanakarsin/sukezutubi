Clifford Taylor IV - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-08-01

Short-form TikTok content creator known for the catchphrase, "I Gotta Go." He is known for sharing sports reaction videos and meme-generated content on his clifford account. He has more than 1.2 million followers on the platform.  

Before Fame

He played tight end for the University of Florida, where he was a walk-on. He never played football prior to playing for the Gators. He made all-conference in basketball for his high school team, where he also served as a captain. 

Trivia

He was a social media personality for the Los Angeles Rams professional football team. He is a social media specialist for the NBA. He also posts on his cliffordtayloriv Instagram account where he has amassed over 200,000 followers. He is the founder of the apparel company Walk-On Gang. He has also become known for the catchphrase "A Win is a Win". He attended both the Super Bowl and NBA Finals after graduating from college in 2021. 

Family Life

He was born in Raleigh, North Carolina. 

Associated With

He has named Scooter Magruder as being one of his biggest inspirations as a content creator.
