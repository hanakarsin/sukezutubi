Mudavadi supporters react to Raila's statement that he will support Kalonzo in 2022

Publish date: 2024-04-21

- Kenyans have voiced their dissatisfaction after NASA flag-bearer said that he will support Kalonzo in 2022

- Raila assured Kalonzo Musyoka that he will be supporting him in 2022 when he (Raila) retires from politics

- A majority of Mudavadi supporters seemed to think that the baton would be given to Mudavadi in 2022

- Raila was speaking after Uhuru Kenyatta asked Kalonzo to abandon NASA for a 2022 deal with William Ruto

Musalia Mudavadi on Tuesday, December 20 divulged information surrounding the conception of NASA- that he birthed it and called Raila and Kalonzo to join in.

The idea was to pool together their numbers to compete favourably against Jubilee's infamous tyranny of numbers.

As reported by TUKO.co.ke, Mudavadi later settled for Premier CS's position with the promise of support from Raila in 2017.

As a matter of fact, on the run-up for the NASA flag-bearer position, Mudavadi claimed that Raila had promised him the ticket.

The ticket never came.

A local daily carried a story on Wednesday, July 5 that Raila had promised to support Kalonzo in 2022.

“Kalonzo will be on the ballot in 2022. We are sure we will form the government and Kalonzo will be the Deputy President. He stands a chance in NASA and we will support him,” Raila said.

Immediately, social media went abuzz with claims that the Mulembe nation has been shortchanged after Kalonzo was handed the baton.

Raila Odinga, if NASA wins in August, plans to be a transitional leader occupying office for only one term and leaving to Kalonzo- his current deputy.

It is still not clear what positions that the rest of the principals including Mudavadi intend to take in 2022 should NASA carry the day at the August polls.
