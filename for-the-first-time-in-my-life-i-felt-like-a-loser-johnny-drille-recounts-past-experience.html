For The First Time In My Life I Felt Like A Loser

Publish date: 2024-08-04

Sensational Nigerian singer, John Ighodaro, popularly known as Johnny Drille, has recounted how he felt after failing to make it to the top 10 of MTN Project Fame.

The singer who recently disclosed that he is married said he felt like a loser after his loss.

Johnny Drille revealed that he featured in the talent show in 2013.

He stated this in a recent interview with reality star KimOprah on Hip TV’s programme, Trending.

According to him, “A lot of people don’t know but I was on Project Fame in 2013. I did not make top 10 not to talk of winning. But for me, it was like the beginning of everything for all these.

“I know when I was evicted that night, something was born in me. For the first time in my life, I feel like a loser. I feel like I wasn’t worth anything. That night, I made a decision that I’m going to become a great person, a great musician, a great artiste. So, it was from then that I started doing music professionally.”

Johnny Drille came into the spotlight in 2015 after releasing his hit song ‘Wait For Me’ under Don Jazzy’s Mavin Records.
