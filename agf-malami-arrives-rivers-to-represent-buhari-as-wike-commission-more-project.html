AGF Malami Arrives Rivers State As Wike Commissions More Projects

Publish date: 2024-07-26

The Attorney-General of the Federation and Minister of Justice (AGF), Abubakar Malami, has arrived in Port Harcourt, Rivers State capital, to witness another project commissioning by Governor Nyesom Wike.

Naija News understands that Malami was in the state to represent President Muhammadu Buhari, who Governor Wike gave a prior invitation.

Security operatives, government officials and media outlets were on the ground as Wike received the president’s representative from his convoy.

See a video clip below: | Source -Channels TV

On Thursday, Governor Wike promised to provide logistics support for the campaign of the presidential candidate of the Labor Party (LP), Peter Obi.

At the commissioning of the Ikoku flyover by the former Governor of Anambra State in Port Harcourt, Wike said Obi has shown that he is a trustworthy politician.

The Rivers governor opined that the LP presidential candidate has all the attributes to be the next President of Nigeria in 2023. “Each time you want to campaign in the state, let me know, all the logistics support, we will give to you.

“I know you as a person; you have all the criteria, you have all the characteristics to lead this country, you have it. Nobody can deny that,” Wike told Obi.
