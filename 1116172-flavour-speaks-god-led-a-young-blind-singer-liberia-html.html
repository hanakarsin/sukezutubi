Flavour speaks on how God led him to a young blind singer from Liberia

Publish date: 2024-07-12

- Many have applauded musician Flavour for his efforts in helping a young singer from Liberia

- The dread locked singer has finally given a reason for his selfless act

- He said it was God who lead him to help the boy

Legit.ng had earlier reported on how popular musician Flavour met a young blind singer named Semah G Weifur during a recent trip to Liberia.

He was so impressed with Semah’s performance that he flew him and his parents to Nigeria and they both worked on a song together.

Flavour has also spoken out about why he decided to help Semah fulfill his dreams. “I believe that God joined mine and Semah’s path together,” he said.
