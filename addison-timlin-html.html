Addison Timlin - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-04-27

Actress most famous for her television roles as Sasha Bingham on Californication and Rachel Lewis on Zero Hour. She has also landed roles in films like Stand Up Guys and That Awkward Moment.

Before Fame

She performed the lead role in a touring production of Annie in 2000. Her first film was 2005's Derailed, where she played the diabetic teenager Amy.

Trivia

She appeared as the titular Juliet in the music video for "Check Yes Juliet" by Florida rock band We The Kings.

Family Life

She was born to Jayne and R.J. Timlin. She has a brother and two older sisters. In October of 2019, she married Shameless star Jeremy White. They welcomed their first daughter Ezer Billie White in October 2018 and their second daughter Dolores Wild White in December 2020. 

Associated With

She played the daughter of Melissa George in the thriller Derailed.
