Paige Mackenzie (TikTok Star) - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-06-28

Superstar on TikTok who has garnered more than 7 million fans to her _paigemackenzie_ account by publishing a mix of comedy, lip-sync and choreographed song and dance videos.

Before Fame

She was about 14 years old when she began to use social media more consistently.

Trivia

She reached 1 million fans on TikTok in October of 2018. Four months prior, she only had 1,000 fans. She shares pictures with friends as well as various pictures from her day-to-day life to her paigexmackenzie Instagram account. 

Family Life

She has an older brother named Corbin. She's been based in Arkansas. Her former boyfriend had a popular TikTok account at jacemcqueary.

Associated With

She used the Jessie J song "Price Tag" for a lip-sync video captioned as "What are your plans for Thanksgiving?"
