Donald Pleasence - Trivia, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-12

British actor who played Dr. Sam Loomis in the Halloween film franchise. He had a major part in The Great Escape, and in the James Bond film You Only Live Twice. In 1978, he won the Outstanding Performance by a Supporting Actor in a Comedy or Drama Special Emmy Award for his performance in The Defection of Simas Kudirka.

Before Fame

During World War II, he was placed in a German prison camp, where he spent time producing and acting in his own plays.

Trivia

He played Prince John in The Adventures of Robin Hood, which ran from 1956 to 1958. He accumulated over 240 acting credits throughout his prolific career. 

Family Life

He was married four times and had five children. His second marriage was to actress Josephine Martin Crombie.

Associated With

He played Blofeld, the arch-nemesis of James Bond in You Only Live Twice, written by Ian Fleming.
