Baringo: 5 killed in car crash while on way home from new year party

Publish date: 2024-07-21

- The accident occurred at 5.30am on Wednesday, January 1, as the deceased were heading home after a party

- The vehicle veered off the road, hit a culvert and landed in a ditch

- They died on the spot and their bodies were taken to a nearby local morgue

At least five people have been confirmed dead after they were involved in a crash along Eldama Ravine-Torongo road in Baringo county after the driver lost control of the vehicle they were in.

The five were from a party that was held at Taiday’s Restaurant where hundreds of residents had gathered to usher in the new year 2020.

According to Eldama Ravine police commander Rashid Mohammad, the accident occurred at 5:30am on Wednesday, January 1, 2010, when the revelers were on their way home.

“They were heading home after the party when the vehicle veered off the road, hit a culvert and landed in a ditch. It is a self-inflicted road accident,” Mohammad said.

All the five occupants in the Toyota Wing Road died on the spot.

Police who had provided security at the party rushed to the scene immediately but found all of them had died.

The bodies were taken to a local county hospital mortuary.

Identity of the victims was not revealed immediately as police were yet to inform the next of kin.
