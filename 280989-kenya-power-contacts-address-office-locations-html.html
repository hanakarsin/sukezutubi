Kenya Power contacts, address, and office locations

Publish date: 2024-07-20

Kenya Power or KPLC, Kenya Power and Lighting Company, is a household name in the country. This company is responsible for supplying electricity through a distribution system that caters for more than 6.675,534 million people in the country. While this company is not the only electricity supplier, it is the main source of electricity for many Kenyans; a reason why a person needs to know Kenya power contacts. Kenya Power was founded in 1922. Its mandate is to facilitate the generation and transmission of electricity. It retails electricity and also creates an efficient network for the distribution of power. The government owns 50.1% of the company and private investors own 49.9%. The company operates under the vision of ensuring that people lead better lives by having power around them. Its core values include integrity, team work, excellence, passion and customer first. The company is committed towards ensuring that it meets the needs of the customers as well as fulfill the expectations of the shareholders.

Besides being the producer and supplier of electricity, this company is also responsible for creating efficient networks for distribution of power. They determine certain things such as the location of the poles.

Kenya power has the duty of ensuring the maintenance of the wiring systems across the country. When natural disasters destroy electric poles or human beings vandalize electricity equipment, this company is responsible for repairs or installations of new equipment.

Kenya Power also installs electricity for people in their homes or buildings. For a person to access the electricity provided by this company, they have to contact Kenya power regional offices or the main branch for installation. This comes at a cost but the benefits of having electricity today outweigh the costs of installing and paying for them.

Electricity is a powerful tool but can also pose danger to human beings. Kenya Power therefore offers tips on how individuals can protect themselves from such dangers. It also offers advice on how electric energy can be save so that people can minimize the amount of money they spend on it.
