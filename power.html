'Power' Latest News and Updates

Publish date: 2024-06-30

Created by Courtney A. Kemp and Curtis "50 Cent" Jackson, Power is an American crime drama series that tells the story of drug dealer and nightclub owner James St. Patrick, who goes by the nickname "Ghost," and is trying to leave the criminal world behind. Ghost struggles with balancing his two lives which include his family, his best friend and partner Tommy Egan, and his love interest Angela Valdes, who also happens to be a criminal prosecutor.

Spinoffs of Power include Power Book II: Ghost (2020), Power Book III: Raising Kanan (2021), Power Book IV: Force (2022), and Power Book V: Influence (TBD).

The final episode of Power aired on February 9, 2020.
