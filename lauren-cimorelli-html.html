Lauren Cimorelli - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-06-02

Pop and R&B artist who is a member of the all-sister group Cimorelli. She was the second-youngest member of the original group, then became the youngest after the departure of Dani Cimorelli in 2020. 

Before Fame

She started performing and recording songs with her sisters at age nine. 

Trivia

She helped Cimorelli release their Made in America EP in 2013. 

Family Life

She grew up in a large family of eleven siblings. 

Associated With

She is joined in Cimorelli by her sisters Christina, Amy, Lisa and Katherine.
