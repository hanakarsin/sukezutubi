Melinda Clarke - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-20

Television actress best known for her daytime drama role on the popular soap opera Days of Our Lives. She has also appeared on the series CSI: Crime Scene Investigation, Nikita, and Vegas. 

Before Fame

After appearing in Days of Our Lives, she starred on Soldier of Fortune, Inc. 

Trivia

She played Julie Cooper-Nichol on the hit teen soap-opera The O.C.

Family Life

She married Ernie Mirich in 1997. The couple welcomed a daughter named Catherine. They later divorced in 2005. 

Associated With

She had a main role on the TV series Nikita, which also starred Shane West.
