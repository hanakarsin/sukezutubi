Death Why? Another Nigerian Celebrity Laments As She Loses Loved One

Publish date: 2024-04-23

Media personality and popular female tiktoker, Beauty Goddess has lost her younger sister to the cold hands of death.

The news was gathered after the media personality took to her Instagram page to post a black image with the caption:”Death why? Ending this month in a painway way. October was really cursed with death Omo.”

She also shared photos of special moments with her sister saying ‘Rest in peace, my little angel.’

According to reports the remains of the child have been moved to the morgue.

Davido Loses Son, Ifeanyi

Meanwhile, the son of internationally renowned Nigerian singer, Davido, Ifeanyi Adeleke, has reportedly died.

Naija News gathered that the little child is rumoured to have died after drowning in a swimming pool.

The three-year-old drowned in a private swimming pool at the family’s residence in Lagos, according to a family source.

“The boy was inside water for too long and they pulled him out and rushed him to the hospital,” a family source said.
