Dan Smyers - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-19

Best known as one half of the country duo Dan + Shay, he rose to fame with the 2013 release of his band's United States Country Music Chart #7-ranked hit "19 You + Me." His group's debut album, Where It All Began, was released in 2014.

Before Fame

Before pursuing a music career, he studied finance at Carnegie Mellon University.

Trivia

Soon after meeting, he and his musical collaborator Shay Mooney wrote a song that was optioned by the country band Rascal Flatts.

Family Life

He grew up in Wexford, Pennsylvania, but later settled in Nashville, Tennessee. In November 2016, he got engaged to Abby Law. They got married in May 2017.

Associated With

His duo opened for country singer Hunter Hayes on Hayes' 2014 tour.
