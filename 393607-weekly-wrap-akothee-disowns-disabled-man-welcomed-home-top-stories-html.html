Weekly wrap: Akothee disowns disabled man she welcomed home and other top stories

TUKO.co.ke has compiled all the top entertainment stories that made headlines within the week.

1. Victor Kanyari's sister gifted car by elder brother days to her birthday

Remember the days when birthdays involved candles, decorations and a cake? Things have changed.

Nowadays, birthdays are marked with new cars, new houses or trips to expensive destinations and many more.

Self-christened pastor Victor Kanyari’s sister Starlet Wahu was given the ultimate princess treatment by her brother a few days to her birthday.

The 21-year-old told her Instagram fans that her big bro knew he would not be around for her big day so he chose to give her a present earlier. It was a car!

2. Nelson Havi enjoys daddy-daughter moment with his baby

Parents pose with their eight children in beautiful photo

Fathers and daughters have a unique bond. Women who have a good relationship with their fathers are lucky enough to look back at their fond memories when they grow up.

In fact, when fathers are present in their daughters' lives, girls grow up with a healthy sense of who they are.

They are also more confident and self-assured and have a clearer understanding of what they want in life just like the Law Society of Kenya President Nelson Havi's daughter has depicted.

The lawyer shared a photo of himself and his young daughter having a father-daughter moment on Friday, November 20, on his Twitter handle and there was nothing short of adorable.

In the photos that clearly spoke more than a thousand words, dad and daughter were happy to be in each other's company.

3. Teacher raises eyebrows as she celebrates birthday at school in wedding dress

Wedding dresses are beautiful and expensive and the fact that you only get to wear them once just does not sit right with some women. One such lady is a teacher at an unknown primary school.

Pictures of her in a beautiful white tubed wedding gown were shared online by a Twitter user and Mzansi artist, Daniel Marven.

In the pics, the teacher, who was celebrating her birthday in a classroom also held a beautiful lace umbrella which completed the "just married" look.

Daniel, who was just as confused as anyone would be, wondered why the teacher decided to wear the wedding gown to school on her birthday, even questioning what was wrong with some teachers.

4. Akothee abandons disabled man after nasty fallout: "Let him go back to the streets"

Singer Akothee has abandoned a young disabled man, Shadrack Mwita, who she had earlier rescued from the streets and given a home.

In a series of lengthy Instagram posts, the single mother went on a rant sharing reasons why she clashed with Mwita leading to their fallout.

Akothee started by saying she has realised that not every needy person requires help as it is business for others, adding she felt stupid for reaching out to everyone who needs help.

"Not everyone on the streets needs help, accommodation or medication - this is purely business for others. I am beating myself for having a big stupid heart to talk, help and reach out to everyone whom I think needs my help."

The singer said welcoming Mwita to her home led to a strained relationship with his brother and disclosed the man lied to her about being 18 years old.

5. Maureen Waititu shows off her handsome sons in vibrant pose

It is the joy of every mom to see their kids grow to be adorable and handsome little beings and this is the case with YouTuber Maureen Waititu.

The single mother of two lit up the internet with a cute photo of her sons with Frankie 'Just Gym It' Kiarie.

The Youtuber captioned the photo with three powerful words saying:

"Two little brothers."

6. Walk the talk: Starehe MP Jaguar shows off his pricey Jaguar ride, posh home

Starehe MP Charles Njagua alias Jaguar is living up to the famous nickname he bestowed upon himself during his days as a top musician in the country.

What better way to live up to his tag than getting himself the Jaguar automobile which is one of the most admired top of the range cars in the world.

The legislator shared a stunning photo on his Instagram account on Monday, November 23, showing off what appeared to be a Jaguar XJ ride, dark blue in colour, 'chilling' at the parking lot.

The model premiered a couple of years back and is one of the hottest rides in the MP's garage with a market price of approximately KSh 8.7 million, according to truecars.com.

7. Man fails job interview after rude behaviour towards receptionist who turned out to be hiring manager

Going for an interview is often a nerve-wracking situation that leaves one with a ball of nerves.

While going for an interview may translate to sitting down with a hiring officer and answering questions, sometimes the interview is deeper than that and starts the moment you walk into the building.

This became so clear after a woman narrated the sad tale of a candidate who blew his chance to get a job in the first five minutes of walking into the office after he dismissively interacted with the receptionist, the Mirror reported.

According to the lady, the receptionist said hello to the man but he seemed uninterested in engaging in a friendly conversation with her.

"He made no eye contact and clearly showed no interest in speaking with her," the woman narrated.
