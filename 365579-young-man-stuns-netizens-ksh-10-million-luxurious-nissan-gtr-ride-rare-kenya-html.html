Young Nairobi man stuns netizens with KSh 10 million luxurious Nissan GTR ride

Publish date: 2024-07-26

- Photos of the expensive Nissan GTR recently stunned Kenyans on social media

- Many wanted to know who owns the car since is it is one of its kind and among the few if not the only one in Kenya

- TUKO.co.ke has learnt the GTR is owned by one Williammm Kimm

The next time you try to say "Kenyans are broke," kindly retract your statement because while you are busy playing 'katii' with your pans in your bedsitter, some people are busy blowing their money in their mansions and whatnot.

This was brought to life recently after photos of a luxurious Nissan GTR was spotted cruising in the streets of Nairobi.

When photos of the pricey grey GTR first hit the interwebs, Kenyans were bewildered and wondered who the owner could be since it is one of the most expensive rides.

Quite a number of people concluded the owner could probably be a politician, celebrity or a blessed Kenyan from a wealthy family.

With the confusion, TUKO.co.ke dug deeper to find out more about the rare car as well as the owner.

In a quick search, it was discovered the ride is not owned by a politician but a very private man of Asian descent known as Williammm Kimm on Instagram and who lives in Kenya.

Well, Kimm may be private but rolls with people on his lane judging by several cars which have since been spotted alongside his, believed to be his friends'.

TUKO.co.ke found out he is a very private person whose IG is even on lock 24/7.

In case you are in the dark, Kimm's Nissan GTR, believed to be the 2019 edition is worth KSh 10 million and is among the few if not the only one in the country.
