The Kenyan government has disappointed its citizens many times that most have lost hope in it.

Growing up, our parents taught us to fight against bad vices such as corruption in the country but even with the many efforts, no change was experienced.

With that, many lost hope and gave up and are today teaching their offspring the reality about the intensity of corruption and how some government officials have been stealing from the public coffers shamelessly.

Kenyan rugby sevens player Dennis Ombachi recently took it upon himself to teach or rather show his infant son how the government will be eating his money when he grows up, lol.

In a hilarious video he shared on Twitter on Wednesday, January 6, Dennis is seen lying on a bed while eating and playing with his son.

He then takes a piece of the food and tries to feed his son who refuses, in a move that saw the rugby player directing the same towards his own mouth and down his intestines.

In essence, he hilariously meant the government will eat everyone's money whether they like it or not.

''Showing him how the government gonna do him and his taxes! Samaki mkunje angali mbichi,'' Dennis captioned his video.
