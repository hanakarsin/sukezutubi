Liza Koshy - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-08-07

Better known by her online persona Lizzza, she has over 17 million subscribers on YouTube and more than 19 million Instagram followers. In 2016, she began starring as Violet Adams in Hulu's Freakish and in 2018 she co-created and starred in the web series Liza on Demand. 

Before Fame

She first gained a following on Vine, where she amassed more than 5 million fans posting comedic videos. She began attending college in the Fall of 2014, deciding to study business marketing.

Trivia

She was named Breakout Creator at the 2016 Streamy Awards. In 2017, she won Choice Web Star: Female at the Teen Choice Awards. She was named the host of the Nickelodeon reboot of Double Dare in 2018. She voiced one of the main characters, Zipp Storm, in the 2021 animated film My Little Pony: A New Generation. 

Family Life

Her real name is Elizabeth Shaila Koshy. She is half Indian and half caucasian and she was born and raised in Houston, Texas alongside her two older sisters, Olivia and Rachel. She was in a relationship with David Dobrik from 2015 to 2018. 

Associated With

She uploaded a Vine called "How girls check out guys vs. How guys check out girls" which featured Tasia Alexis, Lance210, Alex Holtti, Crawford Collins, Jake Paul and Boris Laursen.
