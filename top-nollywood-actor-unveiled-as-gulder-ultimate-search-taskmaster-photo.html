Top Nollywood Actor Unveiled As Gulder Ultimate Search Taskmaster [Photo]

Publish date: 2024-03-17

The organisers of the soon-to-return popular Nigerian reality television adventure show, Gulder Ultimate Search (GUS), have unveiled their taskmaster.

Naija News reports that Nollywood actor and former winner of the reality show Kunle Remi would act as the taskmaster for this year’s edition.

Remi, who was the winner of the 7th edition of the show in 2010, made the announcement via a video on his verified Instagram page.

He captioned the video, “#GulderUltimateSearch is back and I’ll be your taskmaster. Are you ready to go on this journey with me? Have you answered the call? Follow @thegulderultimatesearch and register now at www.gulderultimatesearch.ng.”

Kunle Remi

Recall that the Nigerian Breweries had on September 1st announced that the popular show would return.

It also revealed that this year’s show would be themed ‘The Age of Craftsmanship’.

The reality had started in 2004, running for eleven seasons before stopping in 2014.

To apply, interested participants have to be Nigerians between 21-35 years old.

Applications should be submitted to www.gulderultimatesearch.ng between September 1, 2021, and September 8, 2021.

Shortlisted applicants will undergo a regional selection process in Abuja and Enugu on September 13 and 14, while the selection will take place in Lagos on September 16 and 17, 2021.

The show will air on DStv and other local stations across the country. However, the host of the show is yet to be unveiled.
