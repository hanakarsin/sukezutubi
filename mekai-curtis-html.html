Mekai Curtis - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-04-28

Well known for his series regular role as Fish on the Disney XD series Kirby Buckets, he also landed a role in the Disney film Alexander and the Terrible, Horrible, No Good, Very Bad Day. In 2018, he began starring as the character Cooper on the Brat series Overnights. 

Before Fame

He appeared in a Walmart commercial early in his career.

Trivia

He appeared in an episode of the revival of Arrested Development in 2013.

Family Life

He was born in New Jersey and moved to California for acting. He has a younger sister.

Associated With

He had a guest-starring role in an episode of Girl Meets World, a spin-off of the beloved Boy Meets World starring Corey Fogelmanis.
