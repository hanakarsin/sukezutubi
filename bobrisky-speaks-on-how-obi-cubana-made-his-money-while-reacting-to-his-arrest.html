Bobrisky Speaks On How Obi Cubana Made His Money While Reacting To His Arrest

Publish date: 2024-02-04

Nigerian crossdresser, Bobrisky has reacted to the arrest of billionaire businessman, Obi Iyiegbu, popularly known as Obi Cubana’s by the Economic and Financial Crimes Commission, (EFCC).

Naija News reports that the Economic and Financial Crimes Commission had arrested billionaire businessman, Obinna Iyiegbu, popularly known as  Obi Cubana, on Monday.

It was gathered that Obi Cubana arrived at the office of the commission in Abuja on Monday afternoon and was interrogated by operatives of the anti-graft agency.

Obi Cubana

According to reports, his case borders on alleged money laundering, tax fraud  and abuse of naira notes. 

Reacting to the development, the Nigerian male barbie pledged his unwavering support for the businessman, revealing he worked so hard to amass the wealth he has today. 

He also added that Obi Cubana is a very strong man, one who he’s strongly behind. 

In his own words, ‘One thing you all should notice about me is I don’t kiss people ass, I am not even following him, but i will stand my ground that @obi_cubana is a strong man who work so hard to where he is today. We are strongly behind him.’
