BBNaija Reunion: 'If That Conversation Was About Arin, Would You Have Shut It Down?

Publish date: 2024-04-08

Former Big Brother Naija ‘Shine Ya Eye’ Housemate Angel Smith tackled Nini on Friday night during the reunion show.

Naija News earlier reported that the show started with a clip of Maria talking to other housemates about Angel’s lifestyle toward other male housemates.

While Maria was insisting she didn’t use a derogatory remark at her, Angel expressed anger at Nini that she couldn’t shut down the particular conversation about her despite being friends.

Angel quizzed Nini if she would have kept quiet if such a conversation was about Arin her friend.

She said: “If that conversation was about Arin, would you have shut it down?”

Nini responded that she would have because the conversation was about Angel being strategic in the reality show.

While Maria was attempting to clarify the issue, Angel paid deaf ears as they both talked back at each other.
