We've Made Progress To Rescue Abducted Students, Staff

Publish date: 2024-08-12

The executive Governor of Niger State, Abubakar Bello, on Friday morning, promised to renovate the structures in Government Science Secondary School, Kagara.

Governor Bello, made this promise while addressing newsmen during a press conference which was monitored by our Naija News.

He, however, stated that it is not the turn of Government Science Secondary School, Kagara to be renovated as there are many other schools in the state under construction.

Bello noted that the bandits had easy access to the school because it was not fenced, adding that most of the schools in the state are not fenced.

Recall that bandits in the early hours of Wednesday killed while one student and abducted 27 students, staff, and other persons from the Government Science Secondary School, Kagara.

Nigerians had condemned the state government over the state of things in the facilities of the school which looked abandoned.

Bello, while speaking on the rescue mission, noted that a lot of steps have been taken and the students will be released in a few days or in a few hours.

The Niger State Governor added that he can’t disclose the decisions that had been taken towards the release of the students and staff.

He added that he would work with local vigilantes, traditional rulers in the state to help in the insecurity issues in the state.
