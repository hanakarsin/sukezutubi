Things will be very bad, Kenya will never be the same again- Raila warns

Publish date: 2024-02-12

- CORD leader has promised that Kenya will never be the same again if the election is rigged on August

- He was backed by his deputy and the Mombasa county governor who said that things would be very bad if Jubilee rigged the coming General Election

- Raila previously stated that he had received an intelligence report that Jubilee is registering Ugandans and Ethiopians to vote for Uhuru in 2017

If the election is rigged in 2017, things will be so bad that Kenya as we know it will never be the same again.

This is according to the opposition leader, Raila Odinga who has dared the government to try and rig the election

“Let them try, let them try, I dare them. Kenya will never be the same.” Raila warned.

His sentiments were echoed by his second-in-command who stated that the government was hatching a plan to rig the election in 2017.

“They have turned KDF officers, NIS, police and civil servants into Jubilee campaigners. Let them be warned that we are taking their names and will reveal you. Don’t dare steal votes and if you do, we will deal with you ruthlessly. Things will be bad,” Joho told the residents of Mtwapa.
