Lukaku, Silva, De Jong, Vlahovic, Hojlund, Maguire, And Others

Publish date: 2024-03-22

West Ham United want to sign Manchester United and Three Lions of England’s Center-back Harry Maguire, 30, on loan in this summer transfer window, according to the Telegraph.

In exchange for Portugal’s Bernardo Silva, 28, moving to Barcelona, Manchester City may make an offer for 26-year-old Dutch midfielder Frenkie de Jong, the Mirror reported.

Tottenham Hotspur is considering a deal for Serbian striker Dusan Vlahovic, 22, who plays for Juventus, while the future of Harry Kane is uncertain, according to R. Galetti.

Khvicha Kvaratskhelia, a 22-year-old Georgian winger playing for Napoli, is the subject of a rumored £82 million bid from Newcastle United, according to Corriere dello Sport.

The Glazer family may have to consent to the signing of Rasmus Hojlund, a 20-year-old Danish striker for Atalanta, due to worries that Manchester United’s value may drop during takeover negotiations if they do not add a fresh number nine to the lineup, the Express claimed.

Wolvesplans to make a bid of more than £20 million for Bristol City’s 19-year-old English midfielder Alex Scott, the Telegraph reported.

The 33-year-old English midfielder Jordan Henderson, who is the captain of Liverpool, has a personal contract with Al-Ettifaq, but the two parties have not yet reached an agreement, according to Fabrizio Romano.

Gabon striker Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang, 34, is ready to transfer from Chelsea to Marseille and is willing to take a wage cut to make it happen, France’s objective claimed.

Italian midfielder Cesare Casadei, 20, is wanted by Leicester City on loan from Chelsea, the Telegraph claimed.

Riyad Mahrez, an Algerian winger for Manchester City, is sought after by Al-Ahli of Saudi Arabia, according to Romano Fabrizio.

Allan Saint-Maximin, a 26-year-old French winger for Newcastle United, is also being contacted by Al-Ahli about a transfer, Alkass claimed.

Romelu Lukaku, a 30-year-old striker for Chelsea and Belgium, will no longer be joining Inter Milan, who have opted against making a deal, and his most likely destinations now are Juventus or a club in Saudi Arabia, according to Romano Fabrizio.
