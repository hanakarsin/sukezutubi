Demetrius Flenory Jr. - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-06-23

TV actor known for playing his own father, Meech, in the documentary series Black Mafia Family which premiered on Starz in September 2021. He gained fame in 2022 for appearing in one episode of the hit TV series Euphoria. He played the character Travis. 

Before Fame

His first acting role was on BMF in 2021. He was studying business at UNLV when he found his calling in acting.

Trivia

He is known as the rapper Lil' Meech, and has YouTube music videos, including "Paranoid" and "Bad Habits." He played the character Syl in the 2022 movie Good News. He also earned credit as a producer on the Black Family Mafia series.

Family Life

His father is Demetrius Flenory, or "Big Meech," who was one of the leaders of the Black Mafia Family, a drug trafficking and money laundering cartel. He was sentenced to 30 years in prison. 

Associated With

He is closely linked to the rapper 50 Cent, who helped to move him to Los Angeles and get acting classes.
