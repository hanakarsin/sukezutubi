Trickytee, Laycon, Dorathy, Ozo Nominated For Eviction

Publish date: 2024-06-14

Laycon, Trickytee, Dorathy and Ozo have been nominated for possible eviction from the Big Brother Naija (BBNaija) reality television show.

Naija News reports that this means Nengi, Neo and Vee have made it to the final week of BBnaija.

In a new nomination style, Biggie divided the housemates into two groups of team white – Laycon, Trickytee, Vee and team black – Neo Dorathy, Ozo.

Each team was asked to nominate two housemates from the other group for possible eviction.

Team white nominated Ozo and Dorathy while team black nominated Laycon and Trickytee.

Nengi had earlier emerged the ninth Head of House (HoH) automatically exempting her from the nomination process.
