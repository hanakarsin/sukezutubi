Atiku Holds Strategy Meeting With PDP Governorship Candidates (Photos)

Publish date: 2024-05-21

The 2023 presidential candidate of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP), Atiku Abubakar on Saturday met with the governorship candidates of the party.

Naija News reports the meeting held at the Abuja residence of Atiku and also had in attendance the Vice Presidential candidate of the PDP, Ifeanyi Okowa.

Speaking on what was discussed at the meeting, Atiku said they strategized on how to mobilize Nigerians to vote out the All Progressives Congress (APC) during the 2023 general elections.

He wrote on Twitter: “Today, I met with the governorship candidates of the @OfficialPDPNig at my residence in Abuja. It was an opportunity for to x-ray the state of the nation and how as one we can mobilise Nigerians for the task of ousting the ruling party and commencing the rebuilding of Nigeria.”

2023: Miyetti Allah Speaks On Presidential Candidate It Will Support

The Miyetti Allah Cattle Breeders Association of Nigeria (MACBAN) says it has not taken a final decision on the candidate the group will support for the 2023 presidential election.

The Secretary of the group, Usman Ngerzema disclosed that the congress would meet and determine which of the candidates will get the block votes of its members or if they will remain neutral this time around.

According to Ngerzema, MACBAN consists of members with diverse political affiliations and the decision on which candidate or party to support has not been finalized.

He said they would have to consider the manifesto, capabilities and other things before reaching a final decision.
