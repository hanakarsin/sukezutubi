Kumail Nanjiani And Timothee Chalamet Accidentally Wear Same Suit--React Appropriately

Publish date: 2024-02-16

The same suit mix up at the Gotham Awards was hilarious.

In some circles, the biggest embarrassment you can think of is showing up in the same ensemble as a friend...or rival. But stars Kumail Nanjiani and Timothee Chalamet showed how to keep such a faux pas classy, after both showing up to the Gotham Awards in matching burgundy velvet suits. Luckily they didn't let that slow them up and embraced their wardrobe mix up.

Instead of being embarrassed, the duo found an awesome way to just roll with it, which isn't surprising considering stand up comic and actor Kumail Nanjiani's sense of humor.
