Football coach and radio host Ghost Mulee has reacted to the trending story of Kitui Man David Musyoka, who was bitten by a black mamba that fell from the grip of an eagle's claws.

Earlier, TUKO.co.ke reported the man's incident after explaining that he was driving when he suddenly heard something fall on the roof of his vehicle.

As he slowed down to find out what it was, the snake entered into the car from the passenger's door and bit his hand when he wanted to engage a parking gear.

He got out of the vehicle with the snake still clinging to his hand before onlookers helped him to kill it.

While contemplating what to do with the dead black mamba, an eagle that had dropped it, flew by and picked it up again.

Mulee appeared in an interview with radio personality and comedian Jalang'o on his YouTube channel..

"Even if someone plays with a Kamba person, this one is serious. How do you explain that? If I was that guy, I would go to church every day. I would have killed goats and cows for people just to get rid of that bad omen. Because how do you make sense of that?''' he said.
