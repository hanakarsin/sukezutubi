Vee Mocks Popular Fashion Stylist, Toyin Lawani Over Error On Hermes Outfit At Wakanda Forever Pr

Publish date: 2024-05-02

Former Big Brother Naija Lockdown finalist, Vee has reacted to a mistake made by popular Nigerian fashion stylist, Toyin Lawani, on Hermes outfit to the Wakanda Forever premiere.

Naija News reports that Big Brother Naija stars stormed the event which was held in Lagos on the 6th November, 2022  with various Afrofusion daring outfits for the movie premiere.

However, the outfit of Hermes caused a stir on social media following the misspelling of late Chadwick Boseman’s name as ”Chad Boswick” on the outfit.

Reacting to the error, Vee, via her Twitter handle, noted that people are embarrassed to see the way late Chadwick Boseman’s name was spelt, saying that the outfit was supposed to honor his death and not the other way around.

She wrote: ”Chad Boswick has been killing me all day.” 

Wakanda Forever premiere

The exclusive premiere of highly anticipated sequel to 2018’s Black Panther, Black Panther: Wakanda held on Sunday, November 6th with top Nollywood entertainers and BBNaija’s stars bringing out their inner ‘Wakanda’ spirit to the event.

Some cast and crew, including stars like Letitia Wright, Lupita Nyong’o, Danai Gurira, Tenoch Huerta Mejía, Winston Duke, as well as the director, Ryan Coogler were present for the movie premiere.

Priscilla Ojo, Enioluwa, Adunni Ade, Sharon Ooja, Elozonam, Prince Enwerem, and Allysyn pulled off their looks.

Also spotted at the premiere were Idia Aisien, Omowunmi Dada, BBNaija’s Sheggz, Bella, Hermes, Modella and more.
