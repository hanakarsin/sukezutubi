How to create a website for free step by step

Publish date: 2024-02-02

Websites can be very useful in accomplishing many different projects and especially in marketing products of business to their customers. For individuals, websites provide important avenues for interactions on various levels and for various reasons. Necessarily, the importance of websites cannot be overstated. Creating your own website may seem like a difficult venture. However, creating your own website is amazingly easy. Learn how to create a website for free step by step in this easy guide.

In today’s world, even the smallest of businesses should have websites. With the extremely easy process and low cost of creating a website, it is difficult to understand why some companies do not have websites. Proliferation of the internet and globalization has made having a website a necessity and not a luxury. Having a website is one of the most important marketing strategies for businesses. Here are easy instructions on how to create a website for free step by step.

How to create a website

First step: Choose the platform on which you want to create your website

The first thing on how to create a website is choosing the website platform whereon you would want to build your website. Although there are many content management systems, choosing the most appropriate is of utmost importance. Missing on this first step may cause you to run into problems. In case you are a beginner and are not familiar with coding language, choose a platform that is suitable for starters. Among the factors to consider in choosing a platform include user friendliness, customer support, free, mobile friendly and powerful. Step two: Get a domain name

For you to get customers flowing to your site, you must choose a domain name that is both catchy and relevant. Although the platforms listed above can host your website, you only get to choose a sub domain name that comes across as professional. As such, you need to choose a plan that falls within your budget without compromising on the performance of your website.

Choose user-friendly domain names that have no numbers and hyphens, and be careful not to infringe on copyrights. Choose domain names that are catchy, short and easy to remember.

Step three: Customize and design your website

The final step involves designing your website and making it look good and professional. Depending on the platform you chose, you will have access to many free and premium themes at your disposal. The options allow you to customize your site to meet your preferences.

How to create a website for free

In case you are wondering how to create a website for free, it is as easy as a five-step process. Here are the steps.

  • Choose a website builder than best suits your specific needs.
  • Choose a sub-domain
  • Select a free template.
  • Customize the template by deleting unnecessary widgets, pages, and sections while adding the ones you want.
  • Update your website with the necessary content; blog posts and images.
  • The final step is publishing your website to make it live on the internet.
