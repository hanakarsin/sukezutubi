A police officer has been sentenced to 20 years in prison by a High Court sitting in Naivasha for killing a fisherman eight years ago.

According to a report by The Standard, police constable Evans Maliachi Wiyema was in February 2021 found guilty of killing fisherman Moses Kinyanjui Wanyoike.

Kinyanjui Wanyoike was fishing in Lake Naivasha

Wanyoike was reportedly fishing in Lake Naivasha, Nakuru county, when he met his death.

However, while delivering his ruling, Justice Richard Mwongo indicated that Wiyema could serve ten years if he attend a three-year rehabilitation programme.

Gem MP gets KSh 5 million in a defamation case

In other news by TUKO.co.ke, a Facebook post has earned Gem MP Elisha Odhiambo KSh 5 million after he was defamed by a politician in Siaya county identified as Booker Ngesa.

A court told Ngesa to pay Odhiambo, a first-time MP, the monies as general damages in the defamation case.

The High Court sitting in Siaya on Wednesday, July 28, directed Ngesa to pay the lawmaker following a Facebook post.

The Orange Democratic Movement (ODM) lawmaker had sued the politician, who is also a resident of Gem, for defamation.

The MP told the court Ngesa wrote defamatory content about him and shared it in public.

He said one of his constituents sent him a text message on the phone alerting him that someone had posted something discrediting his name on social media.
