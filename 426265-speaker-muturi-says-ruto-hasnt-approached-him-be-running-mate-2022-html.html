Speaker Muturi Rubbishes Claims Ruto Has Approached Him to Be Running Mate

Publish date: 2024-07-04
  • Speaker Justin Muturi said neither William Ruto nor any other presidential hopefuls had approached him for the running mate slot
  • The National Assembly speaker said he is currently focusing on his presidential bid
  • Muturi said in due time, he will announce the exact party he will use in his race to state house

National Assembly Speaker Justin Muturi has rubbished claims Deputy President William Ruto has courted him to be his running mate in the 2022 General Election.

This came amidst reports that the deputy president's think tank was considering Muturi among other political big wigs from the vote-rich region to bolster his presidential bid.

However, speaking on Spice FM on Wednesday, September 15, the presidential hopeful said he was focused on his campaigns as the race to succeed President Uhuru Kenyatta rages on.

"I have not been approached by anyone to be their running mate. Right now, I am focused on what I would want to do for the country. I am not concentrating so much on what other people want,” he said.

The Mt Kenya spokesperson said in due time, he will announce the party he will use in his race to state house.

This came amidst heightened speculation that DP is considering leaders such as Muturi, Governor Anne Waiguru, Gatundu South MP Moses Kuria, Mathira lawmaker Rigathi Gachagua and former Laikipia East MP Mwangi Kiunjuri for the running mate slot in 2022.

Running mate headache

A few weeks ago, the second in command hinted at picking a runningmate from the Mt Kenya region to solidify his support.

This was after Tanga Tanga leaders from the Mt Kenya region pressurised the DP to consider the region for the running mate slot in order to be assured of the over 6 million voters.

The DP, according to reports, agreed with the leaders but asked for more time to consult so that he could come up with a list of potential candidates to pick from.

Ruto is said to have told his Mt Kenya allies that he would name the running mate a few weeks before the 2022 General Election.

On Tuesday, September 13, a section of Mt Kenya politicians issued ultimatums to presidential hopefuls saying the running mate slot is non-negotiable.
