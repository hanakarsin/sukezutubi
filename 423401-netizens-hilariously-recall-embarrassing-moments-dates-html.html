First dates can be good or bad depending on the person you meet. Some people are treated well on their first dates and fall in love immediately, but others regret going on that date, especially if their dates are unromantic, ill-mannered, arrogant.

Most of the time, first dates are for introduction and learning more about your dates' characters before deciding to take things to another level.

A post on Love Matters Kenya Facebook page invited social media followers to share their first date embarrassments, and the reactions were crazy. Men to women shared their worst first date experiences.

Here are the reactions

"He took me to church, we even got prayed for, thought he was a keeper. Vitenge nishashona kwa mind...so after church we went to his house, and he left to go buy food only to realize that he had gone to meet another woman. He came back after almost two hours and while using his phone to take photos, I saw a message from a woman pop up on his phone. I left," a Facebook user narrated.

Another user said: "Traveling from Kiambu to Mombasa to meet a pregnant lady."

"I wasn't familiar with the cutlery, so the way I placed them showed I was full, yet I had only taken two bites, they took the food away, you can imagine the embarrassment and the way the food was sweet, I had to go to the washroom to wipe my tear, breathe in and out. I will never forget," a female user said.

Sharing his experience, a man said:

"I waited for more than three hours for that daughter of Jezebel, and she still came late, but who am I? I told her am still waiting for her, when she arrived at the place we were supposed to meet, I had already left."

Another guy said:

"I had left Nairobi for Bomet County to meet the daughter of Jezebel who had paid my transport. She noticed I had a torn boxer and bought me two new ones. I almost got a heart attack."

A lucky woman met her prince charming on her first date and sharing her experience, she said:

"The guy promised that he will never disappoint me. I thank God because we are happily living together as a family. In anything you do, always put God first."

A disappointed lady said:

"Travelling from isiolo to Tanzania only to meet my fellow lady."

Another woman said:

"I went to meet a 50-year-old man only to discover he was around 70 years old. I just walked across the hotel and walked out. I was so embarrassed.

Another guy said:

"I slept like never before en snored soundly, thus forgetting that we were two on the same bed. May God forbid, although the relationship ended immediately we woke up."
