Colin O'Donoghue - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-17

Irish television and film actor known for his roles in the horror films Storage 24 and The Rite. In 2011, he began playing Captain Hook on ABC's Once Upon a Time. 

Before Fame

He lived in Paris for a month as a teen to learn French. 

Trivia

He played Duke Philip of Bavaria in a season 3 episode of Showtime's The Tudors. 

Family Life

He married Helen O'Donoghue in 2009; they had a son and a daughter together. He also has a brother named Allen who is an author. 

Associated With

He appeared opposite Ginnifer Goodwin on Once Upon a Time.
